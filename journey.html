Price Ranges.

Our pricing is designed to be transparent, fair, and easy to understand. Our charges are primarily based on the distance traveled, ensuring that you only pay for what you use. We calculate fares using a base rate along with a per-kilometer charge, which may vary slightly depending on the type of vehicle and city. There are no hidden costs—what you see is what you pay. In addition, we strive to keep our rates competitive while maintaining high-quality service standards. Whether you’re traveling a short distance within the city or planning a longer journey, RoadCity offers affordable pricing that fits your needs. We also provide fare estimates before booking so you can plan your trip with complete confidence and clarity.
At RoadCity, our pricing is designed to be simple and transparent, now available in US dollars for your convenience. For short city rides up to 5 km, fares start at approximately $1.20. For distances between 5 km to 15 km, we charge around $0.15 per kilometer, offering an affordable option for everyday travel. For medium-distance trips between 15 km to 50 km, the rate is reduced to about $0.12 per kilometer, giving you better value as your journey gets longer. For long-distance travel beyond 50 km, the cost drops further to approximately $0.10 per kilometer, making extended trips more economical. Please note that these prices may vary slightly depending on the type of vehicle, location, and demand, but we always strive to maintain fair and transparent pricing for all our customers.
